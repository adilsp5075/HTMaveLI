I know you heard this story many times in your life…

But it's my portfolio and I have to say it again and again……

I was an Asura King who rules Kerala, not like other Asuras I was a kind and generous king. I ruled without discrimination, and my people were honest, healthy, and happy under my rule I also temporarily possessed the amrita (nectar for eternal life) obtained by the asuras. The amrita allowed my associates to bring me back to life after my death in one of the wars between Deva and asuras. I was, thus, immune from death. After many wars, I had won heaven and earth. The Devas approach Vishnu to save them. Vishnu refused to join the war or kill his devotee Mahabali(That’s me). He used a tactical approach instead and incarnated as the dwarf Brahmin avatar, Vamana. While I was performing Ashvamedha Vedic sacrifices to celebrate his victories and giving away gifts to everyone, Vamana approached me and asked for "three steps of land". I granted him the gift. Vamana then metamorphosed into Vishnu's giant Trivikrama form, taking all of heaven in one step and earth in second. I realized that the Vamana was none other than Vishnu and offered my head for the third step. So I was taken to padhalam. But the happiest thing is Vishnu granted me a boon whereby he could return to earth every year. So my people celebrated those days as Onam.
